What Is NCO Kit?

NCO Kit is a free, web-based toolkit built specifically for Army Leaders. It uses AI to automate the most time-consuming administrative tasks NCOs face daily — counseling documentation, NCOER bullet writing, fitness test scoring, and soldier management.

The app runs entirely in your browser. No downloads, no CAC required, no Army network dependency. It works on your phone, your personal laptop, or any device with internet access.

Current Features
📋 DA 4856 Generator AI-assisted counseling form generator. Type rough notes in plain language — the AI rewrites them in proper Army regulatory language and generates a printable PDF.
⚡ NCOER Bullet Builder Generates Army-standard NCOER bullets from plain language. Select MOS and rating category, describe what the soldier did, get properly formatted bullets instantly. Includes category fit advisory.
🏋️ AFT Scorer Live score calculator using official June 2025 tables. All 10 age bands, M/F standards, combat and general MOS, and all 4 alternate aerobic events. For personal tracking and preparation only.
👥 Soldier Roster Track your formation's counseling status. Add soldiers, record last counseling dates, and see current/due/overdue status at a glance.
🎖️ Awards Writer Generates ranked accomplishment bullets and IPPSA-ready narrative citations for AAM, ARCOM, MSM, BSM, and LOM. Includes package strength score and specific advisory on how to improve for the award level. Grounded in AR 600-8-22.
💾 Save Feature Logged-in users can save counselings, bullet sets, award citations, AFT scores, and soldier records — accessible from any device via the My Saves tab.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is NCO Kit free?
Yes. The core tools — DA 4856 generator, NCOER bullet builder, AFT scorer, and soldier roster — are free with up to 10 AI generations per month. Premium ($8/month) unlocks unlimited AI, Awards Writer, and Promotion Board Prep Tool.

Is my data secure? Will the Army see it?
NCO Kit is not connected to Army systems, IPPS-A, EES, or any official Army network. Your data is stored securely and is only accessible by your account. Do not enter classified or CUI information.

Can I use this on my phone?
Yes — NCO Kit works on any device with a browser. You can also install it to your home screen like a native app using the "Install NCO Kit" prompt at the bottom of the page.

Is NCO Kit endorsed by the Army?
No. NCO Kit is an independent tool built by an NCO for NCOs. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Department of the Army. All generated documents should be reviewed by the responsible leader before official use.

How do I share NCO Kit with my squad?
Just send them to ncokit.com — no installation required. Works on any phone, tablet, or computer.

Administrative Tools for Army Leaders

NCO Kit is a free AI-powered web application built for Army Leaders. It automates the most time-consuming administrative tasks in daily Army life — from writing NCOER evaluation bullets to generating DA Form 4856 developmental counseling forms. No installation required. Works on any device.

NCOER Bullet Builder

Generate Army-standard NCOER evaluation bullets by MOS. Describe what the soldier did in plain language and get properly formatted bullets for the Character, Presence, Intellect, Leads, Develops, and Achieves sections of the NCOER. Includes automatic category fit advisory based on AR 623-3 and FM 6-22.

DA 4856 Counseling Generator

Generate DA Form 4856 developmental counseling forms with AI-enhanced language. Type rough notes and the AI rewrites them in proper Army regulatory language. Supports initial counseling, monthly performance counseling, event-oriented counseling, and corrective training. Generates a printable PDF.

Army Fitness Test Score Calculator

Calculate AFT scores using the official Army Fitness Test scoring tables effective June 2025. Supports all five AFT events — 3-Rep Max Deadlift, Hand-Release Push-Up, Sprint-Drag-Carry, Plank, and Two-Mile Run. Covers all 10 age bands, male and female standards, combat MOS and general MOS standards, and all four alternate aerobic events for profiled soldiers.

Awards Recommendation Writer

Generate Army award citation bullets and IPPSA-ready narrative citations for AAM, ARCOM, MSM, BSM, and LOM. Accomplishment bullets are ranked strongest first. Includes a strength score and specific advisory on how to improve the package for the award level. Grounded in AR 600-8-22.
